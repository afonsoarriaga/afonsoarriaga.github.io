Attending PAKE 2025

PAKE 2025 will take place in Luxembourg City on February 7, 2025 at 29 Av. John F. Kennedy, 1855 Kirchberg, Luxembourg (Metz/Nancy room).
🧭 Map: By clicking on the map, you can find marked points of interest: the venue, relevant transportation stops, the list of recommended hotels, and a youth hostel for your stay during PAKE 2025 in Luxembourg City.
PAKE 2025 - Google My Maps
ALT
🚌 Transportation: All public transport within the country is free. This includes trains, buses, trams, and even the funicular railway. Passengers only need to purchase a ticket if they wish to travel first class (for trains) or board a cross-border train. The nearest tram stop to the venue is called Kirchberg, Universitéit. Tram links the Train Station with city centre and then with Kirchberg where venue is located. Google Maps or the Mobiliteit website and apps (search “Mobiliteit.lu” for both IOS and Android) will guide you with public transportation in Luxembourg.
🚕 Taxi: Luxembourg has high taxi fares. You can expect to pay between 30 and 50 euros for a 10-kilometer trip. Taxi rides can be pre-booked (e.g. WebTaxi or Uber) or you can hail a taxi anywhere on the street. In Luxembourg City, you can almost always find taxis in front of Luxembourg Train Station and Luxembourg Airport.
🏨 Hotel: The nearest hotel to the venue is The Coque Hotel, located within walking distance or just one tram stop away.
